# Project Brief

## Project Name
**Indian University Fee Comparison Tool for Western Bangla Education (WBE)**

## Purpose
A mobile-first web application designed specifically for WBE counselors to help Bangladeshi students compare university courses and fees across NIU and Sharda University.

## Target Users
- **Primary Users**: WBE counselors (non-technical staff with limited counseling knowledge)
- **End Beneficiaries**: Bangladeshi students seeking higher education in India
- **Context**: Counselors will use this tool on mobile devices during student consultations

## Core Objectives

### 1. Simplify Course Discovery
- Enable counselors to quickly search and find courses based on student requirements
- Provide intelligent filters (field, degree, duration, fee range)
- Show popular/trending courses for quick access

### 2. Enable Easy Comparison
- Side-by-side comparison of up to 3 programs across universities
- Visual indicators for best value, lowest cost, highest ranking
- Compare based on fees, duration, curriculum, career prospects, rankings

### 3. Accurate Fee Calculation
- Automatic scholarship calculation based on student GPA
- Complete cost breakdown (tuition, admission, examination, one-time fees)
- Year-wise payment timeline
- Total investment visualization

### 4. Empower Counselors
- Built-in university profiles with rankings and highlights
- FAQ sections for common student questions
- Ready-made talking points for convincing students
- No technical or deep counseling knowledge required

### 5. Seamless Communication
- One-tap copy of complete fee breakdowns
- Pre-formatted text for WhatsApp/Email sharing
- PDF generation for detailed comparison reports
- Professional output with WBE branding

## Universities Covered
1. **Noida International University (NIU)** - Authorized partner
2. **Sharda University** - Authorized partner with signed MoU

## Success Criteria
- ✅ Counselors can find any course within 30 seconds
- ✅ Fee calculations are 100% accurate with all fees included
- ✅ Works perfectly on mobile devices (320px to 768px)
- ✅ Counselors can copy/share information in under 10 seconds
- ✅ No training required - intuitive interface
- ✅ Offline capability for areas with poor connectivity

## Constraints
- Mobile-first design (primary device: smartphones)
- User has no technical knowledge
- User has limited counseling expertise
- Must work offline after initial load
- Must be fast and responsive

## Future Expansion
- Add more universities (Galgotias, Chandigarh as mentioned by WBE)
- Multi-language support (Bengali)
- Student portal for direct access
- Application tracking system
