# Product Context

## Why This Project Exists

### The Problem
**Western Bangla Education (WBE)** is an education consultancy in Nawabganj, Dinajpur, Bangladesh, serving the northern districts. Their counselors face significant challenges:

1. **Information Overload**: Multiple universities, hundreds of courses, complex fee structures
2. **Manual Calculations**: Calculating scholarships and total fees is time-consuming and error-prone
3. **Student Confusion**: Students struggle to understand and compare options
4. **Communication Gaps**: Sharing detailed information with students/parents is cumbersome
5. **Limited Expertise**: Counselors need help answering detailed questions about rankings, facilities, and career prospects
6. **Mobile Dependency**: All work happens on smartphones, but existing tools aren't mobile-optimized

### The User Journey

#### Current State (Pain Points)
1. Student approaches WBE with interest in studying in India
2. Counselor manually searches through PDFs/brochures for course information
3. Counselor uses calculator to compute fees with scholarships
4. Student asks comparison questions - counselor struggles to answer quickly
5. Counselor manually types/writes fee breakdown to share
6. Student asks about university rankings - counselor doesn't have ready answers
7. Process is slow, prone to errors, and lacks confidence

#### Desired State (With This Tool)
1. Student shares their field of interest and GPA
2. Counselor searches courses in 10 seconds
3. Tool shows relevant programs from both universities
4. Counselor compares 2-3 options side-by-side
5. Tool calculates exact fees with scholarships automatically
6. Counselor taps "Copy" and shares complete breakdown on WhatsApp
7. Student asks "Why Sharda?" - Counselor checks built-in profile for talking points
8. Decision made in one sitting with professional information

## How It Should Work

### Core User Flow

```
[Counselor opens app on phone]
    ↓
[Quick search: "Computer Science" or taps "Popular Courses"]
    ↓
[Sees list: 15 CSE programs from NIU & Sharda]
    ↓
[Applies filters: Bachelor's, 4 years, Budget under 10L]
    ↓
[Results refined to 8 programs]
    ↓
[Selects 3 programs to compare]
    ↓
[Comparison view shows: Fees, Duration, Rankings, Highlights]
    ↓
[Enters student GPA: 4.0]
    ↓
[Tool calculates: Year-wise fees with 50% scholarship]
    ↓
[Taps "Copy Breakdown"]
    ↓
[Shares on WhatsApp with student]
    ↓
[Student asks: "Which university is better?"]
    ↓
[Counselor checks University Profile tab]
    ↓
[Reads: Rankings, Placements, Infrastructure]
    ↓
[Confidently answers student's questions]
```

### Key Features by Priority

#### P0 (Must Have)
1. **Fast Search**: Find any course within seconds
2. **Accurate Fee Calculator**: All fees included, scholarship auto-applied
3. **Copy to Share**: One-tap copy of complete information
4. **Mobile Optimized**: Works perfectly on small screens
5. **University Profiles**: Rankings, highlights, talking points

#### P1 (Should Have)
1. **Comparison View**: Side-by-side comparison of 2-3 programs
2. **Filter System**: By degree, field, duration, fee range
3. **Visual Indicators**: Best value, lowest cost highlights
4. **FAQ Section**: Common questions with ready answers

#### P2 (Nice to Have)
1. **PDF Export**: Generate comparison reports
2. **Offline Mode**: Work without internet after initial load
3. **Popular Searches**: Quick access to trending courses
4. **Bookmark**: Save favorite programs for quick access

## User Experience Goals

### For Counselors
- **Confidence**: Never feel lost or unsure during consultations
- **Efficiency**: Complete consultations 3x faster
- **Professionalism**: Share polished, accurate information
- **Learning**: Build knowledge through built-in content
- **Ease**: No training required, intuitive from day one

### For Students
- **Clarity**: Understand exactly what they'll pay
- **Trust**: Receive professional, accurate information
- **Speed**: Get answers quickly during consultation
- **Comparison**: See multiple options clearly
- **Transparency**: No hidden costs or surprises

## Success Metrics

### Quantitative
- Average search time: < 30 seconds
- Comparison completion: < 2 minutes
- Copy/share time: < 10 seconds
- Tool usage: Daily by all counselors
- Student satisfaction: > 90% find information helpful

### Qualitative
- Counselors feel confident during consultations
- Students receive consistent, accurate information
- Conversion rate improves (more students enroll)
- Counselors recommend tool to colleagues
- Fewer errors in fee calculations
